Winslow is a beautiful boutique salon located in downtown Kirkland, Washington on Market Street.

Our stylists have come together from various places and experiences, but with the same common thought:

To provide our clients with the highest level of service, style that focuses on the individual, and an atmosphere of comfortable luxury.

Our clients are the reason we are here -
without you, this wouldn't be possible!
